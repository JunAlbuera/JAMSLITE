What is a solar light?

What does solar lighting mean?
Simply put, solar lighting is indoor/outdoor lighting powered by the sun. A solar panel collects solar energy during daylight hours and charges a battery that powers a fixture light when the sun goes down.

JAMSLITE is just an innovation of a solar light.

JAMSLITE or Just Another Mini Solar Lite is a DIY solar light; a controller with only a TO-94 chip and a current limiting resistor, a 5V solar panel, a lithium-ion battery and an LED bulb.
